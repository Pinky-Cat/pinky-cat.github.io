Foreword

As an extension to the foreword in Awesome Touhou and the wishes expressed in The Independent Web of 2hu - A Dream of Eastern Turnpikes, this index will catalog any parts of the web that contain any noteworthy Touhou content. The index also acts as a record keeper for when Touhou content on the web is forgotten as new Touhou content gets created and as social networks change or breakup. Hopefully, we see Touhou not become forgotten like the rest of Gensokyo was.

Shopping

  • Booth - Has every type of fanwork from Japan. There a lot of free stuff here too.
  • Gensokyo Store - US Store for buying Touhou albums from Japan in the US.
  • Fumo Shopping Guide - Shopping Guide on Fumos.
  • DizzyLab - Chinese store for music albums.

Games

  • DLSite - Japanese software shop.
  • Suruga - Secondhand shopping source.

  • Touhou PC-98: The Ultimate Collection - A source for PC-98 Touhou games
  • Itch.io - Hub to the Touhou Game Jams.
  • Touhou Curator - Curator of Touhou games on Steam.
  • Backlogged Touhou Project & Fangame Collection - A list of Touhou fangames of a variety of sources and genres.

  • Visual Novel Database - Has a catalog and links to many Touhou visual novel releases.

Art
