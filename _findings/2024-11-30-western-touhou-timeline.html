A history about the community and events that occurred within the Western English-speaking Touhou community from 2004 until today. It is in no way comprehensive of every single major acitvity in the Western community, but this timeline should serve as a reminder about the past on how this community grew.

2004/04/01

Maidens of the Kaleidoscope

The earliest known English Touhou web page. It was a fan page on Touhou that also launched the earliest form of the Maidens of the Kaleidoscope forum.

2005/05/01

Touhou Wiki

Touhou Wiki was first hosted on pooshlmer.com. The Touhou wiki then moved to Wikia on August 2008. Then Touhou Wiki moved to their own servers on November 2010 due to issues with Wikia.

2006/01/30

Gaku Gaku Animal Land

Active from 2006 to 2019, they were the main scanlation group for Touhou doujinshi. Their releases were posted at Gensokyo.org They used Magic Archive Voile as a forum for scan uploads and scanlation feedback. At 2019, Gensokyo.org disspeared.

2007/09/01

The first issue of Another Dream is released

A circle that published Touhou fanbooks that featured artists and writers.

2007/09/02

Create.swf is released

A flash program to customize Touhou characters. Its ease of use for fanwork creation led to the Walfas community in the form of comics, videos, and art.

2007/10/10

Gensokyo.org is online

A news site on the Touhou community, with focus mainly on on Gaku Gaku Animal Land's releases and other English translations for doujinshi/games.

2008/01/08

Touhou-project.com is online

An image board where Touhou CYOA are written.

2010/08/08

Dai Sukima Dan

Released two in-development Touhou visual novels. Eastern Starlight Romance - Eternal Dilemma and Touhou Mecha: Hell's Rising

2011/03/16

Gensokyo Radio Launches

A web radio player for Touhou music, making it more accessible to listen to Touhou music.

2011/06/30

Distant Phastasm

An online Touhou music event / community aimed at overseas arrangers from around the world.

2012/02/26

Touhou Tuesday Broadcasts for the First Time

A radio show ran by Overcoat that broadcasted Touhou music and hosted Touhou community members on the show sometimes.

2012/06/16

thpatch is open to the public.

thpatch is the wiki frontend thcrap's language patches for the Touhou games.

2013/01/01

Distant Union

A circle of artists that released fan comics as physical releases.

2013/09/28

AWA Touhou Scroll Project

70 artists worked to create a scroll featuring every Windows-era character as a gift to ZUN at AWA 2013.

2014/09/20

TouhouCon 2014

The first Touhou-only convention to be hosted in the United States. This took place on 2014 and 2015.

2015/01/01

Touhou Super Smash Battles

A Touhou Super Smash Bros fan game developed by circle From Soy Sauce that was going to release on PC and Wii U. However, the developers ran into controversy after running a crowdfunding campaign without receiving premission from ZUN. As for the community, there were much dissussion on the derivative works guidelines related to what is accepted under the context that "doujin" isn't a well-defined concept in the West.

2015/02/11

Story of a Lost Sky v1.0.0 Release

A SRPG fangame in development since 2009. Created by the circle Feathered Melody.

2016/04/28

Danmaku!! is available for purchase

A tabletop card game based upon Bang!! and featured many Touhou-themed cards.

2016/07/16

Redditaisai 2016

A online event at r/Touhou for creators to share their work. A wide variety of fanworks were released at the eventwith art, music, fangames, MMD videos, and even a cocktail mix all included in the event. This event would be repeated yearly until 2019.

2016/12/04

NitorInc is open for collaboration

A Touhou Warioware fangame collab that was open to the public for development of its microgames.

2017/06/26

DB Scans' First Release

A "small two-person team translating Touhou doujinshi exclusively." Essentially the main scanlation group for Touhou doujinshi after Gaku Gaku Animal Land died. All of their translations are on Danbooru, but not every doujinshi gets typesetted, thus published to the usual scanlation websites.

2018/01/04

The first Touhou Fan Game Jam is ran

Gman8r runs the first game jam with 15 games. Later game jams would have the amount of submissions expand, run themes, and have spinoff game jams.

2023/04/29

TouhouFest 2023

The second Touhou convention in the US. This convention is smaller in scale compared to TouhouCon, but the location at the Torrance Cultural Arts Center with its outdoor garden make the convention more focused on the nature of Touhou.

2023/06/23

Welcome to Gensokyo Zine Release

A Touhou fanzine that featured over 100 artists with illustrations, fanfics, and comics. This is the first zine released by Gensokyo Zines.

2023/12/30

Bunbunmaru Extra No.16 International Issue

This was the first issue of Bunbunmaru to expand beyond the Hispanic community and inlude the international community as a part of its showcase.
