As I spend time in the Touhou community, I continue to expand the collection of Touhou literature ranging from manga, fanfictions, zines, visual novel, research and even literature criticism. But despite the amount of Touhou literature out there, I noticed that reviews and discussion of Touhou literature is quite limited online. As such, I have setup a reading log to show off Touhou literature and to add my own discourse about them in an effort to expose the literary side of the Touhou community.

Season 1 (November 2024)

My first forway with the reading log started on the Moriya Shrine forum during November 2024, under the thread Touhou Reading Log, where I try to read and write about a piece of Touhou literature for each day of November. Writing daily was inspired by the NaNoWriMo challenge and acted as an excuse to start reading my massive backlog of Touhou literature I have compiled for the past year.
